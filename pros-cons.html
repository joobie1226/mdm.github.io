Chrome

Pros:It has extensions for your favorite applications. For example, you can embed the spellchecker or AdBlocker. It's the best adaptive variant for working with Google services (Google Disk, Google Documents, etc.) and it works perfectly with these applications, both in computer and mobile versions. As explained earlier, Google Chrome easily syncs your browser story, bookmarks, passwords, and settings on all of your devices when you log in to your Google account. Cons: It doesn't support such useful features as a built-in VPN, cryptocurrency locker, and reading mode. Chrome is known to have privacy issues with occasions of large user data leaks
