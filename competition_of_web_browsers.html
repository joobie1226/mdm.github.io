Google - Chrome

Google Chrome is best known for its compatibility and simplicity when it comes to its user interface. Chrome offers availability across all platforms, and the mobile version of Chrome can sync bookmarks, passwords, and settings from other devices

Apple - Safari

Apple and Safari offer most of their advantages to Apple users, with a large majority of their features being on Apple devices. These features include Apple Pay, Keychain, and allowing users to sign into other platforms through their Apple ID. For subscribers to iCloud+, which is a subscription service offered to Apple users, Safari will hide the user's IP address, which is like a Virtual Private Network or VPN

Microsoft - Edge

Microsoft Edge heavily promotes its helper features and Copilot AI features. Copilot allows the user to receive a summary of a web page that they are browsing, create images and essays based on a text prompt, as well as add detailed information about web pages they are surfing
