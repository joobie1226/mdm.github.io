ALL ABOUT ME!

Hello! My name is Matthew Moore, and I am currently a Junior at Kennesaw State University. I was born in raised in Marietta, Georgia, where I still reside. Outside of school, I enjoy watching football with my friends, playing guitar, and streaming video games on Twitch.

ABOUT THIS PROJECT!

For this project, I decided to go and research the history and evolution of web browsers. I wasn't excited about it at first, but after learning about all the many changes and updates that have happened over the years, when it pertains to the development of web browsers, as well as the comical pettiness that some companies portrayed in the race for creating the best browser, I really enjoyed my time doing the research. Throughout this project, I am looking to push myself to make each milestone a major step up from the last. I am excited for this opportunity to create a website and build my confidence when using HTML and CSS
